event details
Event 1
Opening Ceremony

The Opening Ceremony will set the tone for the event with formal proceedings such as speeches, welcome addresses, and possibly a few performances. This is the official start where key figures or hosts will introduce the event, highlight its purpose, and engage the audience in an exciting, formal way.

Event 2
Imposter Hunt

In this game inspired by 'Among Us,' a team includes hidden imposters who aim to eliminate members before they finish their tasks. Team members must complete their tasks while figuring out who the imposters are. If a member suspects someone, they can call a meeting to discuss and vote out a potential imposter. The goal is for the team to complete their tasks before the imposters take them down.

Event 3
Cipher Quest

Cipher Quest is a thrilling puzzle-solving event where participants race against time to crack codes and decipher hidden messages. Teams will be challenged with a series of ciphers, ranging from basic encryptions to more complex puzzles. Each solved cipher unlocks the next clue, leading to the final grand puzzle. The first team to crack the final code and complete the quest wins! It’s a test of teamwork, problem-solving, and quick thinking. Get ready to dive into the world of cryptography and unravel the mystery!

Event 4
WorkShop

Our workshop is designed to provide hands-on learning experiences in the field of cybersecurity. Participants will gain practical knowledge and skills from industry experts on various topics, including ethical hacking, secure coding practices, and network defense strategies. The workshop will cover both foundational concepts and advanced techniques, offering insights into real-world cybersecurity challenges and how to address them. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, this workshop will help sharpen your skills and keep you updated on the latest trends in the cybersecurity landscape.

Event 5
Open Mic

The Open Mic session is your opportunity to showcase your talents and express yourself! Whether you're a poet, comedian, musician, or storyteller, the stage is yours. This informal and engaging platform allows participants to perform and entertain the audience in a relaxed atmosphere. It's a great chance to unwind, connect with others, and enjoy a variety of performances. So, grab the mic, share your creativity, and make your voice heard!

Event 6
Hackthon

The Hackathon is an intensive, collaborative event where teams come together to solve real world problems through innovative tech solutions. Participants will have the opportunity to develop software, tools, or applications within a limited time frame, showcasing their coding and problem-solving skills. This fast-paced environment encourages creativity, teamwork, and innovation. Whether you’re a developer, designer, or tech enthusiast, the Hackathon challenges you to think outside the box and push your limits. Get ready to code, compete, and create something impactful!

Event 7
Model United Nations (MUN)

Model United Nations (MUN) is a stimulating educational event where participants simulate the workings of the United Nations. Delegates represent various countries and engage in debates, discussions, and negotiations on global issues such as international security, human rights, and sustainable development. MUN is an excellent platform to enhance public speaking, diplomacy, and critical thinking skills. Participants will draft resolutions, build alliances, and work towards f inding solutions to pressing global challenges, just like in the real UN!

Event 8
Encryption Escape

Encryption Escape is an exhilarating and immersive event that combines the thrill of escape rooms with the challenge of cryptography. Participants will face a series of puzzles, riddles, and codes, all centered around encryption techniques and cybersecurity concepts. Teams must work together to decode hidden messages, solve complex ciphers, and unlock clues in order to "escape" within a set time frame. It’s a perfect blend of problem-solving, teamwork, and technical skill, testing participants' knowledge of encryption and cryptography in a fun and dynamic environment.

Event 9
Quiz

The Quiz event is an exciting competition designed to test participants' knowledge across a range of topics, including cybersecurity, general knowledge, and current events. Teams or individuals will face off in multiple rounds of challenging questions, where quick thinking, accuracy, and depth of knowledge are key to success. This engaging and intellectually stimulating event promises to spark competitive energy and bring out the best in each contestant, with prizes awaiting the top performers.

Event 10
Seminar

The Seminar is a focused and informative session featuring expert speakers who will share their insights on current trends, challenges, and innovations in cybersecurity and related fields. Participants will have the opportunity to engage with industry leaders, gain valuable knowledge, and ask questions to deepen their understanding of critical topics. This event aims to foster learning, discussion, and networking among attendees, making it a vital component of the overall program. Whether you are a student, professional, or enthusiast, the Seminar promises to enhance your expertise and inspire new ideas in the realm of cybersecurity.

Cultural Night

Cultural Night is a vibrant celebration showcasing the rich diversity and heritage of various cultures through performances, food, and activities. Attendees can experience a variety of traditional dances, music, and art, highlighting the unique customs and traditions of different communities. This event aims to promote cultural understanding and appreciation while providing a fun and engaging atmosphere for participants. Whether through culinary delights, interactive exhibits, or captivating performances, Cultural Night offers a memorable experience that fosters connection and unity among attendees, making it a highlight of the event. Join us for a night of exploration, celebration, and joy!

Event 12
Box Cricket

The Game Zone/Box featuring Cricket is an engaging area designed for sports enthusiasts and casual players to enjoy thrilling cricket matches. Participants can join friendly games, practice their skills, or simply watch the action unfold in a vibrant atmosphere. Equipped with all necessary gear, this zone encourages teamwork, sportsmanship, and friendly competition, making it a perfect place for both seasoned players and newcomers to connect and share their love for the game!

Event 13
Catch The Flag (CTF)

"Catch The Flag" (CTF) is an exciting cybersecurity competition where participants engage in solving security-related challenges to capture digital flags hidden within various tasks. This event encourages teamwork and collaboration as teams work together to crack codes, exploit vulnerabilities, and navigate complex scenarios. CTF fosters hands-on learning and practical application of cybersecurity skills, making it an invaluable experience for aspiring cybersecurity professionals. With a range of difficulty levels, participants can enhance their knowledge while competing for prizes and bragging rights in a fun, dynamic environment!
